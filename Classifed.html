Subject

Operation Final Descent: Neutralization of Victor Krieg

Executive Summary

On December 30, 2024, at 12:01 PM UTC, operatives from Black Talon Defense Solutions (BTDS) successfully executed a covert operation to neutralize Victor Krieg, CEO of Krieg Systems International (KSI). The operation involved the commandeering of the DDG-10 Destroyer’s weapons systems and the targeted destruction of Krieg's H125 Helicopter. The operation resulted in the confirmed death of Victor Krieg and all personnel aboard the helicopter.

Operational Details

Target: Victor Krieg

Location: DDG-10 Destroyer / H125 Helicopter

Timeline:

  • 11:45 AM UTC: BTDS operatives infiltrated the DDG-10 Operations Room.
  • 12:00 PM UTC: Control of the GIM-6 CIWS weapon system secured.
  • 12:01 PM UTC: SAM missile launched, destroying H125 Helicopter.

Confirmation

The operation was verified through photographic evidence, ISR drone footage, and intercepted communications. Victor Krieg was confirmed to have been aboard the H125 Helicopter at the time of its destruction.

Strategic Implications

The elimination of Victor Krieg destabilizes Krieg Systems International, reducing their influence in global defense markets and operations. Additionally, the operation strengthens Aberian leverage in the ongoing corporate cold war between Aegis Global Solutions and Krieg Systems International.
