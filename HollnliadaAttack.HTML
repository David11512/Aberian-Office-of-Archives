Classified Report: Hollnliada Attacks

Classification Level: Alpha-1 – Top Secret

Report ID: AO-GWOT-START

Date Filed: February 10, 2025

Subject

The Hollnliada Terrorist Attacks: Catalyst for the Global War on Terror (GWOT)

Incident Overview

On the early morning of **February 3, 2025**, two commercial aircraft were hijacked by members of the extremist group **Crimson Resolve**. At **5:45 AM**, the first aircraft, a Hollnliada Aircharter A320, crashed into the **Aberian National Assembly** building, resulting in its complete destruction and the loss of **700+ government officials, staff, and civilians**.

At **6:15 AM**, the second aircraft, an Aberian Airlines Boeing 777-200LR, struck the headquarters of the **Department of Counterterrorism and National Intelligence (DCNI)**. This attack decimated the nation’s counterterrorism leadership, killing over **300 key personnel**, including top intelligence officers.

Timeline of Events

  • 4:00 AM: Both aircraft departed from Hollnliada International Airport under routine conditions.
  • 5:00 AM: Radar operators detected unusual deviations in flight paths but initially dismissed them as system errors.
  • 5:45 AM: The first aircraft struck the Aberian National Assembly, igniting a massive explosion.
  • 6:15 AM: The second aircraft crashed into the DCNI headquarters, resulting in catastrophic damage.
  • 6:30 AM: Emergency response teams began evacuations and rescue efforts at both sites.
  • 7:00 AM: Supreme Chancellor David Evans was evacuated to a secure location aboard **Airforce One**, accompanied by two E4B “Doomsday Planes.”
  • 9:00 AM: Aberliada declared a national state of emergency and ordered the mobilization of all armed forces.

Immediate Impact

The attacks resulted in over **1,000 fatalities** and left Aberliada’s government infrastructure in disarray. The destruction of the DCNI headquarters crippled national counterterrorism efforts, leaving the nation vulnerable to further attacks. Public morale plummeted as citizens grappled with the scale of the tragedy.

Response and Retaliation

  • February 4, 2025: Supreme Chancellor David Evans declared the **Global War on Terror (GWOT)**, vowing to eradicate extremist groups worldwide.
  • February 6, 2025: Aberian Air Transport Command deployed troops into Nateland under the guise of peacekeeping operations.
  • February 10, 2025: Aberian forces launched their first offensive against suspected Crimson Resolve strongholds in Nateland.

Strategic Implications

The Hollnliada Attacks marked a turning point in Aberian foreign policy. The immediate militarization and global reach of the GWOT solidified Aberliada’s role as a dominant military power. The use of extreme measures, including nuclear readiness, set a precedent for aggressive counterterrorism strategies.
